About Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is honoured to host its 72nd Annual Conference 2027 in the historic “City of Pearls,” in collaboration with the University of Hyderabad, an Institution of Eminence. This premier international summit serves as a global nexus for librarians, information scientists, academicians, and technology innovators.

As the digital and physical frontiers of information continue to merge, this conference provides a vital forum to examine the shifting paradigms of our field. This landmark event will help redefine the role of libraries as catalysts for global knowledge and social progress.

The main theme of the conference is Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence 2027 (FLARE-2027). The conference focuses on emerging trends including Artificial Intelligence, Green Libraries, Digital Transformation, Research Data Management, and Sustainable Development.

The sessions will explore innovative technologies and the evolving role of libraries in empowering research, education, and knowledge societies.

About University of Hyderabad

The University of Hyderabad, established in 1974, is one of India's premier institutions for higher education and research with a beautiful 2,500-acre campus.

Recognized as an Institution of Eminence, it offers multidisciplinary education and is internationally known for academic excellence, innovation, and research.

About Indira Gandhi Memorial Library

The Indira Gandhi Memorial Library is the central library of the University of Hyderabad supporting teaching, learning, and research through extensive print and digital collections.

The Library provides modern digital services, scholarly databases, plagiarism support, research assistance, and advanced infrastructure for users.

About Indian Library Association (ILA)

Founded in 1933, the Indian Library Association is India's premier professional organization for library and information professionals, promoting education, research, innovation, and collaboration.

Through conferences, journals, workshops, and advocacy, ILA continues to strengthen the library movement and professional excellence across the country.
