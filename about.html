About FLARE-2027

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is proud to host its 72nd Annual International Conference 2027 in Hyderabad, in collaboration with the Indira Gandhi Memorial Library, University of Hyderabad.

The conference provides a national and international platform for librarians, information scientists, academicians, researchers, and IT professionals to discuss emerging trends in library and information science.

Conference Theme

Transforming Library Services in the AI Era: Library Services, User Engagement and Community Impact

About University of Hyderabad

The University of Hyderabad, established in 1974, is one of India’s leading institutions, recognized as an Institution of Eminence. It is known for academic excellence, research innovation, and multidisciplinary education.

About Indian Library Association (ILA)

The Indian Library Association, established in 1933, is the premier professional body of library professionals in India. It promotes library development, training, research, and professional collaboration.
