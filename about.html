About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is proud to host its 72nd Annual ILA International Conference 2027 in Hyderabad, in collaboration with the Indira Gandhi Memorial Library, University of Hyderabad.

This international event brings together librarians, information scientists, educators, researchers, policymakers, and technology experts from across the globe to discuss the evolving role of libraries in the digital age.

The conference serves as a platform for sharing innovative ideas, best practices, research findings, and emerging technologies that are shaping the future of library and information services.

Conference Theme

Transforming Library Services in the AI Era: Library Services, User Engagement and Community Impact

Why Attend?

Participants will explore Artificial Intelligence in Libraries, Digital Transformation, Open Science, Research Data Management, User Engagement, Sustainability, Knowledge Management, and the Future of Information Services.

The conference offers keynote lectures, invited talks, technical sessions, workshops, panel discussions, poster presentations, and networking opportunities with professionals from India and abroad.

About University of Hyderabad

The University of Hyderabad, established on 2 October 1974 by an Act of Parliament, is one of India’s premier institutions of higher education and research. Recognized as an Institution of Eminence, the University is renowned for its academic excellence, innovative research, and vibrant campus environment.

Situated on a sprawling 2,500-acre campus, the University offers multidisciplinary education and world-class research facilities that attract students and scholars from across India and abroad.

About Indian Library Association (ILA)

The Indian Library Association (ILA), established on 13 September 1933, is the premier national professional body for library and information professionals in India.

ILA promotes library development, professional education, research, training, publications, and advocacy while providing a common platform for librarians, academicians, and information professionals.
