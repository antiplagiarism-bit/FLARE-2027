FLARE-2027 Conference Venue

Indira Gandhi Memorial Library (IGML), University of Hyderabad

📍 Venue Location

The FLARE-2027 International Conference will be held at the prestigious Indira Gandhi Memorial Library (IGML), University of Hyderabad, Gachibowli, Hyderabad.

🏛️ About IGML

The Indira Gandhi Memorial Library is the central library of the University of Hyderabad and a major academic hub supporting advanced research.

  • Fully automated digital library system
  • Large reading halls and seminar spaces
  • Access to international journals & e-resources
  • High-speed Wi-Fi and digital research zones

🎓 About University of Hyderabad

A premier central university known for its green campus, research excellence, and world-class infrastructure.
