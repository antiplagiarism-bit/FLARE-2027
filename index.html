72nd ILA International Conference

Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027)

7–9 January 2027 | University of Hyderabad

About Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is honoured to host its 72nd Annual Conference 2027 in collaboration with the University of Hyderabad.


The conference theme "Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027)" focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Digital Transformation, Sustainable Libraries, Research Support and Emerging Technologies.

Conference Themes

  • Libraries as Drivers of Sustainable Development
  • Artificial Intelligence and Digital Transformation
  • Research Support and Scholarly Communication
  • Assistive Technologies and Inclusive Libraries
  • Smart Libraries and IoT
  • Open Science and Research Data Management
