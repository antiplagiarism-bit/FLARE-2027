About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA), in collaboration with the University of Hyderabad, is organizing the 72nd ILA International Conference on Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027) from 7–9 January 2027 at the University of Hyderabad, Hyderabad, India.

FLARE-2027 aims to provide an international platform for librarians, information professionals, academicians, researchers, students, policymakers, and technology experts to exchange ideas and discuss the latest developments in Library and Information Science.
