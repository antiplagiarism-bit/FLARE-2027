About the Conference

The 72nd ILA International Conference (FLARE-2027) brings together librarians, researchers, academicians, information professionals, policymakers and technology experts to discuss emerging trends in Library and Information Science.

The conference theme, Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence, focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Digital Libraries, Research Data Management, Open Science, Scholarly Communication, Knowledge Management, Information Literacy and Sustainable Library Services.
