72nd ILA International Conference

Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027)

7–9 January 2027 | University of Hyderabad

About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is honoured to host its 72nd Annual Conference 2027 in the historic “City of Pearls,” Hyderabad, in collaboration with the University of Hyderabad, an Institution of Eminence. This premier international summit serves as a global nexus for librarians, information scientists, academicians, researchers, and technology innovators. As the digital and physical frontiers of information continue to merge, the conference provides a vital forum to examine the shifting paradigms of Library and Information Science. This landmark event aims to redefine the role of libraries as catalysts for global knowledge creation, research advancement, innovation, and social progress.

The main theme of the conference is Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027). The conference focuses on a wide range of emerging and trending topics in the field of Library and Information Science, including Libraries as Drivers of Sustainable Development, Green Libraries, Artificial Intelligence and Libraries, Assistive Technologies and Inclusive Library Services, Research Data Management, Open Science, Digital Preservation, Smart Libraries, and other emerging technologies shaping the future of information services.

The sub-themes of the conference encompass many of the latest innovations and transformative technologies in the library profession. Through keynote lectures, invited talks, technical sessions, workshops, panel discussions, and poster presentations, FLARE-2027 will provide opportunities for knowledge sharing, professional networking, and international collaboration. The conference will explore the essential contribution of libraries in supporting research excellence, fostering intellectual exchange, and driving meaningful community empowerment in the digital age.
