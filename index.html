72nd ILA International Conference

Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027)

7–9 January 2027 | University of Hyderabad

About the Conference

The Indian Library Association (ILA) is honoured to host its 72nd Annual Conference 2027 in collaboration with the University of Hyderabad, an Institution of Eminence.

This international conference brings together librarians, information scientists, academicians and technology innovators to explore emerging trends in Library and Information Science.

The theme Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027) focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Digital Transformation, Sustainable Libraries, Assistive Technologies and Research Innovation.

Conference Themes

  • Sustainable Libraries & Green Initiatives
  • Artificial Intelligence in Libraries
  • Digital Transformation & Smart Libraries
  • Research Support & Scholarly Communication
  • Assistive Technologies & Inclusive Services
  • Open Science & Research Data Management
