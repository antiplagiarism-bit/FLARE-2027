Conference Theme

Transforming Library Services in the AI Era: Library Services, User Engagement and Community Impact

Scope of the Conference

The conference invites original research papers, case studies, technical papers and review articles from librarians, information scientists, researchers, academicians, and IT professionals.

Sub-Themes

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Libraries
  • Digital Libraries and Institutional Repositories
  • Open Access, Open Data and Open Science
  • Library Automation and Emerging Technologies
  • Research Data Management and Scholarly Communication
  • Knowledge Management Systems
  • ICT Applications in Library Services
  • Information Literacy and User Engagement
  • Smart Libraries and Digital Transformation

Paper Submission Guidelines

  • Original and unpublished research papers are invited
  • Paper should be in MS Word format
  • Abstract should not exceed 250 words
  • Full paper length: 3000–5000 words
  • APA referencing style should be followed
  • Paper must include Title, Abstract, Keywords and References
