Call for Papers

The Organizing Committee of the 72nd ILA International Conference (FLARE-2027) invites original research papers, review papers, case studies, best practices, innovative projects, and scholarly contributions from librarians, faculty members, researchers, students, information professionals, policymakers, and industry experts.

FLARE-2027 provides a global platform for exchanging ideas on Artificial Intelligence, Digital Libraries, Open Science, Research Data Management, Scholarly Communication, Emerging Technologies, Sustainable Libraries, and Future-Ready Information Services.
