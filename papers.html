Call for Papers

The Organizing Committee of the 72nd ILA International Conference invites original research papers, case studies, innovative practices, and scholarly contributions from librarians, faculty members, researchers, students, information professionals, and industry experts.

The conference provides an international platform for exchanging ideas, showcasing innovations, and discussing emerging trends in Artificial Intelligence, Digital Libraries, Open Science, Research Data Management, and future-ready information services.

Conference Theme

Future Libraries: Advancing Research & Excellence (FLARE-2027)

🌱 1. Libraries as Drivers of Sustainable Development

  • Green Libraries and Sustainable Infrastructure
  • Libraries and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
  • Digital Preservation and Long-term Knowledge Sustainability
  • Carbon-Neutral Library Practices
  • Smart Libraries for Sustainable Communities
  • AI Applications for Sustainable Library Services

🤖 2. Artificial Intelligence and Digital Transformation

  • Artificial Intelligence in Library Services
  • Machine Learning and Intelligent Information Retrieval
  • AI-powered Cataloguing and Metadata Generation
  • Cloud-based Library Management Systems
  • Semantic Web and Linked Open Data
  • Blockchain and Emerging Technologies

📚 3. Research Support and Scholarly Communication

  • Open Science and Open Access
  • Research Data Management
  • Institutional Repositories
  • Bibliometrics, Scientometrics and Altmetrics
  • Research Integrity and Publication Ethics
  • One Nation One Subscription (ONOS)

🏛️ 4. Library Leadership and Policy Framework

  • Strategic Planning for Libraries
  • Quality Assurance and Accreditation
  • E-Governance in Libraries
  • Professional Development and Capacity Building
  • Library Marketing and Advocacy

♿ 5. Inclusive Libraries and Assistive Technologies

  • Accessible Library Services
  • Assistive Technologies for Persons with Disabilities
  • Inclusive Digital Resources
  • Multilingual Information Access
  • Diversity, Equity and Inclusion in Libraries

🤝 6. Collaborative Knowledge Networks

  • Library Consortia and Resource Sharing
  • University–Industry Collaboration
  • Knowledge Management Systems
  • Global Research Partnerships
  • Citizen Science Initiatives

🗄️ 7. Digital Preservation and Cultural Heritage

  • Digitization of Rare Collections
  • Archives and Manuscript Preservation
  • Digital Humanities Projects
  • Long-term Preservation Technologies

⚖️ 8. Legal and Ethical Issues

  • Copyright and Intellectual Property Rights
  • Academic Integrity
  • Plagiarism Detection
  • Data Privacy and Information Ethics
  • Predatory Publishing

📱 9. Libraries and Digital Media

  • Social Media Engagement
  • Digital Marketing of Library Services
  • Information Literacy
  • Misinformation and Fact Checking

📝 Paper Submission Guidelines

  • Only original and unpublished research papers should be submitted.
  • Papers must be written in clear academic English.
  • Include Title, Abstract, Keywords, Main Text, and References.
  • Ensure plagiarism-free content and proper citation of sources.
  • All submissions will undergo peer review.
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the paper.
  • Selected papers may be published in the conference proceedings.

📢 Invitation to Authors

The Organizing Committee of the 72nd ILA International Conference warmly invites librarians, faculty members, researchers, information professionals, policymakers, students, and industry experts from India and abroad to submit original research papers and participate in FLARE-2027.

The conference aims to foster innovation, encourage interdisciplinary collaboration, and explore emerging technologies that will shape the future of libraries and information services.

Accepted papers will be presented during the conference and selected contributions may be considered for publication in the conference proceedings, subject to the review process and editorial policies.
