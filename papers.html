Call for Papers

The Organizing Committee of the 72nd ILA International Conference invites original research papers, case studies, innovative practices, and scholarly contributions from librarians, faculty members, researchers, students, information professionals, and industry experts.

The conference provides a premier platform to exchange ideas, showcase innovations, and discuss emerging trends shaping the future of libraries and information services in the era of Artificial Intelligence, digital transformation, and research excellence.

Paper Submission Guidelines

  • Only original and unpublished research papers should be submitted.
  • Papers must be written in clear academic English.
  • The manuscript should include Title, Abstract, Keywords, Main Text, and References.
  • Authors are responsible for ensuring plagiarism-free content and proper citation of sources.
  • All submitted papers will undergo peer review by the Technical Committee.
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the paper during the conference.
  • Selected papers may be considered for publication in the conference proceedings, subject to editorial review.
