Conference Venue

Venue Details

The 72nd ILA International Conference (FLARE-2027) will be held at Indira Gandhi Memorial Library, University of Hyderabad, Hyderabad, Telangana, India.

The University of Hyderabad is one of India's premier institutions, known for its beautiful green campus, world-class academic environment, and excellent conference facilities.

Location Highlights

  • Modern conference halls with audio-visual facilities
  • Well-connected by road, rail, and air
  • Close to major hotels and accommodation
  • Accessible from Rajiv Gandhi International Airport
  • Metro and public transport connectivity available nearby
